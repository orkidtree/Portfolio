About Me

(born in 2000) A non-binary queer artist based in Davao City. Their artistic practice is rooted in a keen observation of everyday life, they appreciate mundane details and translate them into works of art. Their inspirations come from calm landscapes, contemporary pop culture, and a deep affinity towards animals.
